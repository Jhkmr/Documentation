This website documents the process of creating a hybrid zine which explores ways of expanding the possibilities of post-binary writing in the german language. Utilising web-to-print as a bridge between the analog and digital, the experiments and final zine exploit the capacity of the web as a fluctuating and collective space, thereby attempting to translate the fluidity of language, culture and gender into contemporary areas of typographic use.

Shared Insights

Andrea Dreier is a graphic designer and teacher combining design with education and collaborative practices. Her MA Thesis Beyond asterisks and underlines highlights how the intersection between grammatical gender, social norms, and linguistic habits become visible through type design. Her sharing of resources and insights into the practice have been invaluable to the contextualisation of this project within a larger ecosystem.
